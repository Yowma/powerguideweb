Surge Protection Devices manufactured by Protec Power Solutions Inc. for individual use protection. Applicable for PLC, computers and dedicated equipment protection. With current ratings from 1 to 30 Amperes and voltage ratings from DC to 277VAC. ProS Series has a 25 Year Free Replacement Warranty.

ProS Series has hybrid design incorporating discrete all mode protection and utilizing our encapsulated Sinewave Tracking Circuitry (STC) design to provide lowest possible let-through voltages. All suppression circuits are encapsulated in our high dielectric compound to promote long component life and protection from the environment and vibration. All circuits include component level./thermal fusing.

Protec Power Line SPDs represent the latest in Surge Protection technology, design and performance. Products have been developed to meet all recommended industry standards and filter a wide range of transient wave forms.
