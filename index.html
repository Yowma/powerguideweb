FAQs

Find answers to your most pressing questions about our power quality solutions and services.

What is power quality?

Power quality refers to the voltage, frequency, and waveform of the electrical supply. It affects the performance and lifespan of equipment, ensuring optimal power quality minimizes disruptions and protects your systems.

How do surge protectors work?

Surge protectors work by diverting excess voltage away from sensitive equipment. They absorb and redirect surges, ensuring safety and efficiency, protecting devices from damage. This ensures your equipment remains protected and operational.

What is lightning protection?

Lightning protection systems are designed to safeguard structures from lightning strikes. They provide a safe path for the electrical current, minimizing damage and ensuring safety for people and property.
