What does PowerGuide Solutions specialize in?

PowerGuide Solutions specializes in providing power quality solutions, focusing on the distribution and installation of surge protection devices and lightning protection systems. We protect buildings and personnel from direct lightning strikes and safeguard electrical equipment from voltage surges.

What industries do you serve?

We serve a wide range of industries, including broadcasting, medical, financial, government, call centers, data networking, toll plazas, gas stations, and manufacturing, ensuring reliable power quality for critical operations.

What are surge protection devices, and why are they important?

Surge protection devices (SPDs) protect electrical equipment from voltage spikes caused by lightning, power surges, or electrical disturbances. They’re essential to prevent damage, downtime, and costly repairs, ensuring the longevity of your systems.

How does a lightning protection system work?

A lightning protection system directs lightning currents safely to the ground using air terminals, conductors, and grounding systems, combined with SPDs to mitigate surges, protecting buildings and personnel from harm.

Can you help with both new and existing installations?
